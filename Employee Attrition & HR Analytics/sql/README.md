# Employee Attrition & HR Analytics using MySQL

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition patterns using the IBM HR Analytics dataset. The objective is to identify workforce trends and factors influencing employee turnover.

## Business Questions

* Which departments experience the highest attrition?
* Is employee salary associated with attrition?
* Which job roles have the highest average tenure?
* Which job levels are most at risk of attrition?
* Does overtime contribute to employee turnover?
* Does work-life balance impact attrition?
* Is promotion delay related to employee resignation?

## Skills Used

* MySQL
* Aggregate Functions (COUNT, AVG, SUM)
* CASE Statements
* GROUP BY and HAVING
* Stored Procedures
* Data Import and Validation
* HR Analytics

## Key Findings

* Department-wise attrition rates were calculated to identify high-risk departments.
* Salary bands were analyzed to understand compensation-related turnover.
* Overtime showed a strong relationship with employee attrition.
* Promotion delay was not a major attrition driver in this dataset.
* Job levels and work-life balance significantly influenced retention patterns.

## Dataset

IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance Dataset
